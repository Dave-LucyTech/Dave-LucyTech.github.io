Prevention Maintenance

Preventive maintenance is a proactive service that involves regularly scheduled inspections, cleanings, and adjustments of computer hardware and software to prevent problems from occurring. This service is essential to keep your computer running smoothly and reduce the likelihood of hardware failures, software crashes, and security breaches. Preventive maintenance can include tasks such as virus scanning, software updates, disk cleanups, and backups. By identifying and fixing potential issues before they become major problems, preventive maintenance can save you time, money, and frustration.

Hardware Maintenance

Hardware maintenance is a critical service that involves the repair, upkeep, and optimization of computer hardware components. This service is essential to keep your computer running smoothly and prevent downtime due to hardware failures. Hardware maintenance can include tasks such as cleaning dust from the internal components, replacing damaged parts, and updating drivers and firmware. Regular hardware maintenance can extend the life of your computer, improve performance, and save you money in the long run.
